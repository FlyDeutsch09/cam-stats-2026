Based on FOI 2025 cycle Winter Pool data showing pool offers made by each college.

### Scoring Formula

```
Weighted Raw = (C1_score × 25) + (C2_score × 25) + (C3_score × 20) + (C4_score × 15) + (C5_score × 10) + (C6_score × 5)
Final Score (out of 100) = Weighted Raw ÷ 10
Maximum possible: 100 | Minimum possible: 10
```

## 7. Key Data Insights from Raw Materials

### From the Power BI Dashboard Screenshots (`raw/`)

The `raw/` folder contains 17 files — screenshots (PNG) and PDF exports from the Cambridge admissions statistics Power BI dashboard. Key data captured:

- **2025 & 2026 Engineering Application breakdowns** — show application volumes by college for Engineering
- **2025 & 2026 Engineering Offer Breakdown Forms** — show offer distributions by college and offer type (direct vs. pool)
- **Mainland Engineering Applicants, sorted by college** — directly shows which colleges Chinese Engineering applicants prefer
- **Winter Pool Data for international Engineering applicants** — two files showing pool behavior for international (including Chinese) Engineering applicants
- **IBPG.png** — IB/Pre-U/GCSE grade breakdown, relevant to your IB DP1 context
- **2025 Acceptance breakdown** — shows final acceptance numbers
- **Cambridge Application Data.pdf** — comprehensive 5-page PDF compilation of all dashboard views

### External Data Corroboration

The web research data (FOI responses, Cambridge-published statistics, Chinese education platforms) corroborates the patterns visible in your screenshots:

1. **Engineering is hyper-competitive and getting worse:** Applications rose from 2,654 (2024) → 3,263 (2025) → 3,972 (2026), while offers remained flat (~371 → 393 → 414). The offer rate dropped from 14.0% → 12.0% → ~10.4%.

2. **Chinese applicants face tougher odds:** China mainland Engineering applicants rose from 349 (2024) to 599 (2025), while offers stayed at ~63-66. The Chinese Engineering offer rate dropped from 18.1% → ~11%.

3. **Winter Pool is a real factor:** 28% pool success rate means ~72% of pooled applicants get no offer. Colleges with high pool imports (Girton: 91, Homerton: 84, Fitzwilliam: ~40-45) may have fewer direct-offer slots.

4. **College choice matters less than ESAT + interview:** The Cambridge system normalizes college choice through the pool — but your stated preferences (Chinese community, direct offers, stability) make the college choice important for your QUALITY OF LIFE, not just admission probability.
